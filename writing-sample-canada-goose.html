Writing Sample – Equity Research Report on Canada Goose

In 2017, I participated in a research competition, the challenge was to write a research report on a company or project. I chose to write about Canada Goose’s IPO (when it was still private at the time) in the format of an Equity Research report: to analyze it’s limited pro forma information and guess it’s IPO price and expected performance.

Using a very conservative base case and one comparable peer, Aritzia, my targeted price was $20.00. The closing price on its IPO date was $21.53.

It took me 2 days to research and write this report.
